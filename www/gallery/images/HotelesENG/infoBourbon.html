Bourbon Asunción Convention Hotel

Located in Luque - Gran Asunción is 3 minutes from Asunción International Airport, In addition to being close to roads and waterways, privileged condition in the new center of the capital, especially for the realization of congresses, conventions, fairs and events. As part of the complex is the Conmebol Convention Center with an exhibition area of ​​3,5 thousand m² and capacity for up to 4,500 people simultaneously, and the South American Football Museum, themed space on sport that is passion in South America, which brings together 9 titles in World Cup teams - 5 from Brazil, 2 from Argentina and 2 from Uruguay.
